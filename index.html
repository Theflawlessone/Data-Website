Data Science and Analytics

Data science is an interdisciplinary field that combines statistics, computer science, and domain knowledge to extract valuable insights and knowledge from structured and unstructured data. It involves processes such as data collection, cleaning, analysis, and visualization to make data-driven decisions. Data scientists use algorithms, machine learning models, and statistical methods to identify patterns, predict outcomes, and optimize business processes.

The field has gained significant importance across industries, from healthcare to finance, as organizations strive to leverage data for improving efficiency, innovation, and competitive advantage. With the explosion of big data, data science continues to evolve, playing a pivotal role in shaping the future of technology and business.
